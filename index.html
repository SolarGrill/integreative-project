Product

Our product consists of a grill with a solar energy source, which will be driven through a solar panel integrated into the grill itself. It will be a product ideally made for outdoor barbecues and will greatly help reduce environmental pollution. It will use solar energy which is considered as one of the best renewable energy, so it will be of great function in our product. Most of it it´s made of metal and wire, some of the things it has is a solar panel and a controller.

The change that our product makes is important, since when making meat at high temperatures, with coal or other non-renewable materials, they produce compounds such as Polycyclic Aromatic Hydrocarbons (PAH) and carcinogens known as Heterocyclic Hydrocarbons (HCA) that are inhaled by the people who prepare the products. food. This causes long-term inflammation in the body, increases the risk of some cancers, and is also linked to developmental and reproductive abnormalities. Small Ultrafine Particles (UFP) from smoke can also enter the bloodstream and affect the body's organs, causing Alzheimer's and brain damage. On top of all this, grilling is also an air pollutant. This produces Carbon Monoxide, Benzene, Butadiene, Formaldehyde, etc. That they are not only harmful to health, but also affect our environment. For this reason, our grill uses solar panels, to replace the use of non-renewable materials that affect us in various ways.
